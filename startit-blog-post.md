Da li znaš onaj momenat kada igraš igricu sa ortakom — društvenu, ping pong, bilo koji sport — i odmah ti je jasan karakter čoveka? U igri puštamo naše unutrašnje dete. Ne možemo da se pretvaramo.

Baš zato su **gamifikovani hiring assessmenti** postali jedan od najmoćnijih alata za zapošljavanje. I baš zato ovog vikenda — **25. i 26. aprila u CDT Hub-u** u Beogradu — organizujemo **ZenHire AI Coding Hackathon**: 48 sati da dizajniraš i izgradiš gamifikovani test koji meri kognitivne sposobnosti, skillove i otkriva pravi talenat.

Rok za prijavu: **četvrtak, 23. aprila, do 14 časova.**

## O čemu se radi

Svaki tim dobija jedan jasan zadatak — izgradi igru koja meri jednu konkretnu dimenziju: pažnju, radnu memoriju, multitasking, numeričku obradu, znanje Excela, JavaScripta, ili nečeg trećeg.

Jedna dimenzija. Clean merenje. Game-like iskustvo. Automatski scoring.

Tražićemo rešenja koja:

- **Mere tačno ono što tvrde** — psihometrija nije folklor
- **Imaju engaging gameplay** — ne HR formular sa drugim licem
- **Rade bez bugova** — funkcionalni prototip u 48 sati
- **Pitch-uju se jasno** — 5 minuta, 3 slajda, demo uživo

Timove pravimo od 1 do 3 člana, a balansiramo ih po skillovima: **inženjer + dizajner + psiholog** obično nosi više nego tri programera.

## 🎨 Visual prompt za blog header (5:25 ultra-wide banner)

**Target format:** horizontal wide banner, 5:25 aspect ratio (≈ 2500×500 px), for Startit-style blog hero.

**Gemini / Imagen prompt (copy-paste):**

> A minimalist, editorial-style banner image in ultra-wide horizontal format (5:25 / 5:1 aspect ratio, approximately 2500×500 pixels). Abstract composition of floating geometric game pieces arranged across the full width of the frame in a flowing, zen-like rhythm: frosted glass cubes, glossy spheres, translucent rounded tokens, small tiles with subtle numerals, and softly bevelled shapes. The pieces suggest cognitive puzzle games mid-motion — like a slow-moving conveyor of thoughtful mechanics. Color palette: warm off-white background (#FDFCF8), muted mint green (#2E9D7E), and sandy gold (#D8AF51). Some pieces are frosted translucent glass catching gentle green-gold refractions from soft natural light. Soft natural lighting from upper-left, long subtle shadows, shallow depth of field — a few pieces in sharp focus, others softly blurred into bokeh across the long horizontal axis. Very subtle diagonal line texture in the background. Leave clean negative space in the center-left for optional text overlay. Mood: zen, intelligent, modern, premium, thoughtful, editorial. Style: Apple-keynote-meets-Stripe-design aesthetic, crossed with premium product photography. Negative prompts: no text, no typography, no faces, no hands, no logos, no brand marks, no clutter, no misspelled letters.

**Fallback:** if Gemini produces garbled visuals at this extreme aspect ratio, generate at 16:9 (1920×1080) then crop to 5:25 in Canva/Figma. Add title/logo overlay as text layer in post — don't ask Gemini to render text at this scale.
